How to Perform a Proper Pre-Trip Inspection

Visual Inspection
Always Perform a Pre-Trip Inspection before leaving!
  • Check for any damge on body.
  • Check all lights for damage.
  • Check all windows and mirrors for damage.
  • Check tread on tires.
  • Check Brakes.
